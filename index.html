OPay User Demographics & Analytics Dashboard

Power BI Desktop Large Datasets (1M+ Rows) Data Modeling Cross-Filtering

Project Overview

Developed an enterprise-ready dashboard analyzing 1.77 million individual user profiles from OPay Nigeria (spanning 2022 to May 2025). This dataset exceeded standard Excel capacities (1.048M row limits), leveraging Power BI Engine capabilities to process high-volume information, structure demographic slices, and uncover regional operational trends.

Core Visual & Dashboard Capabilities Implemented

  • Temporal Trend Filters: Configured a Year Slicer (2022-2025) mapping steady linear transaction growth across groups, revealing recurring seasonal anomalies like a regular transaction dip every February.
  • Geographic Slicing Engine: Established interactive maps covering 6 major Nigerian geopolitical zones showing that the South West serves as the primary adoption baseline (35%+ of user density).
  • Demographic Segmentation Matrix: Tracked an exact 50/50 gender balance alongside generational clusters showing that Millennials and Gen X lead active platform reliance, with Gen X keeping the highest densities of local business accounts.
